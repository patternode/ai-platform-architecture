## Exception Process

### Requesting Framework Exceptions
When the recommended frameworks don't meet specific requirements:

1. **Assessment**: Document why recommended frameworks are insufficient
2. **Alternative Evaluation**: Evaluate alternative using standard criteria
3. **Risk Assessment**: Identify risks and mitigation strategies
4. **Approval Process**: Submit to architecture review board
5. **Documentation**: Document decision and create transition plan

### Exception Criteria
- **Technical Gaps**: Critical capabilities not available in recommended frameworks
- **Performance Requirements**: Specific performance needs not met
- **Regulatory Constraints**: Compliance requirements not supported
- **Strategic Partnerships**: Business requirements for specific vendors
