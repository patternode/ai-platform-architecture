# Community of Interest Charter: AWS agentCore & Agentic AI

## Executive Summary

This charter establishes a Community of Interest (CoI) focused on AWS agentCore technologies and agentic AI systems to drive strategic adoption, knowledge sharing, and innovation across BNZ. The community will serve as the primary forum for advancing our agentic AI capabilities while ensuring responsible implementation aligned with enterprise standards.

## Charter Overview

### Mission Statement
To establish BNZ as a leader in agentic AI by fostering collaboration, sharing expertise, and driving strategic adoption of AWS agentCore and related technologies while maintaining the highest standards of risk management and customer value creation.

### Vision
Create an interconnected ecosystem of AI agents that enhance customer experiences, streamline operations, and enable innovative financial services while adhering to regulatory requirements and ethical AI principles.

## Community Scope & Focus Areas

### Primary Technologies
- **AWS agentCore**: Primary platform for agent development and orchestration
- **Amazon Bedrock**: Foundation model management and deployment
- **AWS Lambda**: Serverless agent execution environments
- **Amazon Q**: Business intelligence and code assistance
- **AWS Step Functions**: Agent workflow orchestration
- **Amazon Connect**: Customer service agent integration
- **AWS AppFlow**: Data integration for agent systems

### Secondary Technologies
- **Anthropic Claude**: Advanced reasoning and safety-focused models
- **OpenAI Integration**: GPT model integration patterns
- **LangChain/LangGraph**: Agent framework development
- **Vector Databases**: Knowledge representation and retrieval
- **Real-time Streaming**: Event-driven agent architectures

### Strategic Focus Areas
1. **Customer Experience Enhancement**

   - Proactive service recommendations

2. **Operational Excellence**
   - Intelligent process automation

3. **Innovation & Product Development**
